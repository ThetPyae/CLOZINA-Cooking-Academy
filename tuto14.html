Crema Catalana

Tutorial Video

Ingredients

  • 500 ml whole organic milk
  • ½ an orange
  • ½ a lemon
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 7 large free-range egg yolks
  • 95 g sugar, plus extra to serve
  • 1 heaped teaspoon cornflour

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan pour the milk, and add orange and lemon zest, and cinnamon, then gently bring to boil. Remove from the heat and allow to infuse for around 15 minutes.

  2. Whisk the egg yolks with the sugar and cornflour until the mix becomes pale and creamy.

  3. Pass the milk through a sieve and remove what´s left. Bring back the milk to the saucepan and place it over a medium heat.

  4. Add the eggs mixture gently, just before the milk reaches boiling point, whisking continuously for around 6 to 7 minutes until it begins to thicken. Turn off the heat.

  5. Pour the mix into individual ramekins and cover each of them with cooking paper. Once cooled at room temperature place them in the fridge for at least 3 hours.

  6. When serving sprinkle 1 tablespoon of sugar on top of each crema catalana and caramelize by using a blowtorch or place them under a preheated grill for less than a minute or until the sugar turns brown, and then serve.
