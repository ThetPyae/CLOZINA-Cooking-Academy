About Us

Making is Better Together

Clozina is home to a collective group of curious, driven doers who believe in the power of creating. From our expert instructors to our passionate employees—we’re here to answer your questions, share inside tips and ensure all of our members thrive in our community.


How Can I Contribute?

The Kitchn is written by a small, steady team of freelance writers and we do not at this time accept unsolicited freelance submissions. However, we do hire from time to time, so keep an eye on our Job Listings for future postings. We do not accept unsolicited guest posts. If, however, you are a reader or a blogger who would like to share a kitchen project or recipe link from your own site with our audience, we would love to hear from you! We also are always accepting questions from our readers, which are published in our Good Questions column. Click the link below to contact us with any of these! → Contact us: Ask a question, show us your kitchen, tell us a story about your life in the kitchen! Thank you for reading, and happy cooking!

What We Believe

A calm, healthy, beautiful home is a necessary foundation for happiness and success in the world. Creating this home doesn’t require large amounts of money or space. It requires inspiration, connection to resources and motivation to do something about it. The basic elements of good home design can be learned and achieved by all. Simplicity and luxury are not mutually exclusive.


Our Goal

To connect people to the resources they need to improve their homes, while reducing their reliance on stuff.
